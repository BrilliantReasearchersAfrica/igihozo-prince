About Us

Brilliant Researchers Africa (BRA) is a youth-led African community organization specializing in applied scientific research, circular materials engineering, and functional prototyping. BRA was officially registered as a non-governmental organization in Rwanda on September 4, 2024. It is headquartered at INATEK/Kibungo, Eastern Province. Founded by Eng. Iradukunda Emmanuel (BRA Country Coordinator & Chief Technical Officer), the organization is driven by a passionate team of young engineers and researchers from various technical backgrounds. BRA focuses on 4 key areas: Applied Scientific Research, Functional Prototyping, Circular Materials Engineering, and Disaster Risk & Fire Safety Systems.
